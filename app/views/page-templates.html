Page templates

You can find some example templates for pages in the /lib/example-templates folder within your prototype (or /docs/templates in older versions of the kit).

These include:

  • content pages
  • question pages
  • check your answers
  • a confirmation page

To use one, copy and paste it from there into your /app/views folder.

There are also some templates for common patterns in the NHS service manual.

NHS website pages

If you are prototyping a service which includes pages on the NHS website, you can use these additional templates:

  • NHS website homepage
  • Health A to Z
  • Live Well
  • Mental health
  • Care and support
  • Pregnancy
  • NHS services
  • Coronavirus hub
